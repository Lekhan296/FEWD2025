About Nitte College

Nitte is a well-known educational hub located near Karkala in Karnataka. It is home to the Nitte Education Trust institutions, founded by Justice K. S. Hegde. The campus is situated in a scenic environment surrounded by greenery, offering students a peaceful and inspiring atmosphere to learn and grow.

The institutions at Nitte provide education across various fields such as engineering, medical sciences, management, pharmacy, and more. Among them, Nitte Mahalinga Adyanthaya Memorial Institute of Technology (NMAMIT) is especially recognized for its engineering programs and strong emphasis on research, innovation, and industry connections.

Apart from academics, the campus focuses on overall student development with excellent hostel facilities, modern infrastructure, sports complexes, and cultural activities. Nitte has built a strong reputation for producing skilled graduates who contribute to society while upholding values of discipline and professionalism.
